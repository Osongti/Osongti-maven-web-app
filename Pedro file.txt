A Village Awakens
It was October, and the raining season had just ended in the picturesque village of Nguti, nestled on the fringes of the sprawling Banyang Mbo rainforest in the South West Province of Cameroon. Nguti is a place where the earth breathes with life, and the rainforest stands as a stoic sentinel, sheltering the village with its lush canopy. 
In Nguti, the rains had just recently concluded, the village of Nguti was bustling with activities. The rich, red-brown earth is moist and fertile, promising a bountiful harvest. For the villagers, this is a time of both hard work and anticipation as they prepare for the upcoming harvest season especially for subsistence crops. In Nguti, farming is not just a way of life; it is the very heartbeat of the community
