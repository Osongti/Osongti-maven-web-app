In Nguti, the rains had just recently concluded, the village of Nguti was bustling with activities. The rich, red-brown earth is moist and fertile, promising a bountiful harvest. For the villagers, this is a time of both hard work and anticipation as they prepare for the upcoming harvest season especially for subsistence crops. In Nguti, farming is not just a way of life; it is the very heartbeat of the community.Life in Nguti is steeped in tradition, and this extends to the division of labor.
